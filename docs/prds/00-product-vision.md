# Product Vision

## Goal

Help homeowners and renovators quickly imagine what a remodel could look like before they spend time on contractor outreach, material samples, or furniture changes.

## Product thesis

- People want to see a plausible remodel direction before they commit budget or energy.
- A single room photo plus a short prompt can anchor style, scope, and realism guidance.
- The first result should feel easy and low-friction, but the business should monetize soon after trust is earned.
- The strongest experience blends instant visual inspiration with enough structure to support real decisions.

## Core user promise

Upload a room photo, describe the remodel direction you want, get one complimentary guest preview, and then continue with paid credits once you are ready to iterate.

## Primary audience

- Homeowners considering kitchen, bathroom, living room, bedroom, and curb-appeal upgrades
- Renovators who want quick concept direction before they request bids or choose materials
- Consumers who value visual confidence but do not need professional CAD output

## Monetization

- Exactly one complimentary guest generation before conversion
- The next meaningful action should trigger a combined sign-up and credit-pack purchase flow
- One-time credit packs purchased through Polar are the MVP monetization path
- Social login with Google and Apple plus email/password should be supported for conversion
- Credits should map cleanly to analysis, generation, and regeneration costs so pricing can be tuned over time

## Success criteria

- New visitors understand the value proposition on the first landing-page visit
- Guests complete the first photo-to-result flow without needing support
- A meaningful percentage of users attempt a second action and hit the conversion wall
- Paid users generate multiple remodel variants per project within the first session

## Non-goals for MVP

- Native mobile apps
- Full contractor marketplace workflows
- Measurement-perfect floorplans or CAD-level outputs
- Collaborative designer review features
